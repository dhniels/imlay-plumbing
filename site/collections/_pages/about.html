Imlay Plumbing caters to both residential and commercial clients. We were founded with the goal to be a one-stop shop for all your goals. We are equipped to handle any kind of project, including service, remodels, new construction. We are also now offering excavation and electrical services as well!

We’ve served Iron County and the surrounding areas for over 20 years. We provide a wide range of services for both residential and commercial clients. Our professional team is ready to handle any emergency plumbing need that may arise.

  • Talented team
  • Competitive pricing
  • Quick service
  • Locally Owned & Operated
  • 24/7/365 Service
  • Quality Service Guaranteed!

We serve Iron County Utah and all surrounding areas including: Cedar City, Parowan, Summit, New Harmony, St. George, Beaver, Duck Creek, Brian Head, Panguitch, Kanab, and more.
